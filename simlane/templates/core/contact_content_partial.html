Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my iRacing account?

Visit your profile settings and use the "Connect Racing Platform" feature to link your accounts securely.

Is my racing data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Read our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

Can I delete my account and data?

Absolutely. You can delete your account and all associated data from your profile settings or by contacting us.

Do you support other racing platforms?

We're always expanding! Currently we focus on iRacing, but support for other platforms is planned.
