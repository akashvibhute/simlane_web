Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and plans

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, you'll be charged the prorated difference immediately. When you downgrade, the change will take effect at the end of your current billing period.

What happens if I exceed my member limit?

If you're on the Free plan and exceed 5 members, or on Basic and exceed 25 members, you'll be prompted to upgrade. We'll give you a grace period to either upgrade your plan or remove members to stay within your limit.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. You can explore all the premium features without any commitment. You won't be charged until the trial period ends.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your billing dashboard. You'll continue to have access to paid features until the end of your current billing period, then your club will automatically move to the Free plan.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Currently, we only offer monthly billing to keep things simple and flexible. However, we're considering annual plans with discounts in the future. Stay tuned for updates!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) and debit cards through our secure payment processor, Stripe. All payments are encrypted and secure.
